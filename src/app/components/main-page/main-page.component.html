Learn more about Foodcaring



Hello and welcome to Foodcaring, a place where we believe that everyone should be able to eat a warm meal! Food is life and our application thrives to give disadvantaged people the chance to eat better through your kindness. Donors can choose whom to feed a nice meal, or let the system do that in a smart and careful way. We also make sure that everyone is treated equally by prioritizing persons that have not yet received a meal.
